Fried Rice

Indonesian dish
  • Consume within: 5 days
  • Prep time: 10 mins
  • Cook time: 10 mins

Recipe

Sauce & Chicken

  1. Mix Sauce in a bowl and Pour 2 tsp over chicken
  2. Toss the chicken to coat and set aside for 10 minutes.

Egg

  1. Heat 1 tbsp oil in a wok or skillet over medium heat.
  2. Add egg and scramble until just cooked (still a touch wet) then remove.

Fried Rice

  1. Turn heat up to high, add remaining 1/2 tbsp oil and bacon into the same skillet.
  2. Cook for 1 minute or so until fat starts to melt, then add onion and garlic.
  3. Cook until bacon is light golden, about 1 - 2 minutes.
  4. Add vegetables (still frozen is fine). Cook for 1 minute.
  5. Add chicken and cook until it changes from pink to white - about 1 - 2 minutes.
  6. Add rice and Sauce ingredients. Cook for 1 1/2 minutes, stirring constantly.
  7. Add egg and green onion, toss through to disperse, then remove from heat.
  8. Serve immediately!
