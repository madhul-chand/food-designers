Wonton

Chinese dish
  • Consume within: 3 days
  • Prep time: 20 mins
  • Cook time: 15 mins

Recipe

Wontons

  1. Place Filling ingredients in a bowl.
  2. Use a potato masher to mash until fairly smooth about 20 mashes.
  3. Don’t turn the prawn into a complete paste, small chunks are good.

Wrapping

  1. Use My Way (better Wonton Soup) or the Asian Grocery Store Way (easier to pack for freezing).
  2. Lay Wontons on work surface and Use 2 teaspoons to put the Filling on the wontons.
  3. Work in batches of 5 if starting out, up to 15 or 20 if confident and brush 2 edges with water.
  4. Fold to seal, pressing out air.
  5. Brush water on one corner and bring corners together, pressing to seal.
  6. Place wrapped wontons into a container with a lid as you work (so they don’t dry out).

Cooking

  1. Bring a large pot of water to boil.
  2. Place wontons in water and cook for 4 minutes or until they float.
  3. Remove with slotted spoon straight into serving bowls and ladle over broth.

Broth

  1. Place Broth ingredients in a saucepan over high heat and Add white ends of scallions/shallots if leftover from Wonton filling.
  2. Place lid on, bring to simmer then reduce to medium high and simmer for 5 – 10 minutes to allow the flavours to infuse and remember to pick garlic and ginger out before using.
  3. If using vegetables, blanch in the soup broth and place in serving bowl.

Assemble Soup

  1. Prepare noodles according to packet directions (if using noodles) and place in serving bowl with cooked wontons and blanched vegetables.
  2. Ladle over soup. Serve!
